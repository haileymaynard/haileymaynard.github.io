OK2BX

Creative Director: Andria Kushan
Copywriter: Hailey Harmon
Content Producer/Art Director: Maddie Churchwell
Designer: Taylor Minth

OK2BX stands for “it’s okay to be extraordinary” and they celebrate the beautiful differences that make each of us unique. They call people in, not out, to accept everyone in a bold, bright way. Their mission is to spread kindness, understanding and acceptance.

In the wise words of Natasha Bedingfield: The rest is still unwritten.
